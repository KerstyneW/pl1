Shark sightings along the False Bay Coastline

As part of my Web GIS Development course at Fleming college I was required to complete a Google My Maps introductory tutorial. The tutorial provided step-by-step instructions to create the following map which shows shark sightings at different beaches along the False Bay Coastline in South Africa.

The data provided to complete the tutorial was courtesy of Shark Spotters and the MAPA Project.
